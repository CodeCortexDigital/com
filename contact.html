Do you offer free consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations for all projects. During the consultation, we'll discuss your requirements, provide initial recommendations, and give you a ballpark estimate. There's no obligation to proceed after the consultation.

What information should I provide for a quote?

For the most accurate quote, please provide:
1. Project type (website, mobile app, etc.)
2. Key features and functionality needed
3. Target audience and platform requirements
4. Timeline expectations
5. Budget range if available
6. Any existing designs or references
